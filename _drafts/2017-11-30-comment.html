Fifty people from the Ministry of Testing - Boston came out to Spotify's Davis Square, Somerville, MA office on Tuesday, November 28th to listen to Chris Phair talk about WTF is a Quality Coach? 

Although Chris Phair has been a Spotify Quality Coach for seven months, he has been in the Quality Assurance field for the past twelve years: Nuance for 8 years, and Fitbit -- where I first met Chris -- for two years.

Spotify has a rather unique organizational structure as described in Harvard Business Review's How Spotify Balances Employee Autonomy and Accountability, consisting of self-organizing squads, tribes, and guilds.

But what about QA Engineers? What about the software testers?

Spotify doesn't have any.

As Chris put it, instead of testing being an activity of a few, testing is everybody's job. And to help... they have a Quality Coach that trains and mentors teams to take ownership of the quality process.
